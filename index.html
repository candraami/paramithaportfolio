About Me

Greetings, Ya gurl Mitha's here. In early 2025, I started creating various handmade plushies and floral decor made by flannel or felt cloth and pipe straw as a personal passion project. What began as a small blog to share my crafts slowly grew into this space, while I need to be honest that this wasn't the complete display. I'am still working to reduce the room of improvement over time.

What you see here is no grand palace, but a living gallery. Each piece were threaded as a whispered promise between maker and muse. To create the fabled perfection, every thread is pulled with care, every petal placed as if for the best, because you are. Regardless, may this galleries showcase of pieces made with patience and care could engaged customers as our honorary highness.

Our path connects within the threads,

your humble crafter Paramitha.
